What is ABLX

ABLX is a private electronic platform providing registrants the facility to raise capital and or invest in start-ups and Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Africa through. We believe in using the power of the crowd and social democracy to lift Africa out of poverty, create jobs and spur development by providing capital to Entrepreneurs and SMEs. Whether you are a start-up / SME seeking capital, an Investor looking for financial returns or an agent for catalysing change and development, we provide a platform for you to satisfy your urge!

  • Crowd Funding on ABLX

    Crowd Funding is where capital is collected from a pool of individuals, “the Crowd”. In the case of ABLX, these individuals are registered as 'Investors'. The capital raised is passed on to an entrepreneur / business owner seeking funding to start-up, grow, expand or execute a business strategy. The recipients of capital are registered as 'Entrepreneurs' on ABLX and they list their entrepreneurial venture / business as a ‘Campaign’. Entrepreneurs provide equity / a share / a stake in their business in exchange for the investment received.
    For example 20% in exchange for 10,000 USD. If a pool of individuals invest 100 USD or more each, the target of 10, 000 USD could be reached and even exceeded. Each investor will receive a portion of the 20% in equity in proportion to depending on how much they have committed to the Campaign.

  • Using the ABLX platform

    Using the ABLX platform for equity crowd funding provides a compliment and or supplement to debt capital. By raising capital via Crowd funding on ABLX Entrepreneurs are able to reach out not only friends, family and members of their communities but also to the wider Investor community including High Networth Individuals (HNWIs), Institutional investors and other Retail Investors efficiently and at lower overall costs. In addition to raising capital, Entrepreneurs benefit from constructive criticism and feedback from Investors as part of the due diligence and review prior to capital being committed to the campaign, when a Campaign is fully funded and the capital has been raised, Investors can also contribute by opening doors for greater business sales, providing strategic advice and professional services to the business.
    For an Investor, ABLX provides an opportunity to put your financial, human and social capital towards idea, project that you believe in and gain from it at the same time. On ABLX, Entrepreneurs and their businesses are vetted prior to being listed on ABLX for fact and accuracy of all statements and assertions made in a Campaign.

How it works

Using the ABLX platform for crowd funding provides an alternative to loans, overdrafts and loan brokers with high rates of repayments. For an investor, you could put your money towards a project you believe in and gain from it at the same time. Investors should understand the risksof investing in startups and consider these products as patient capital

  • Investors

    Investors are able to browse all listed campaigns sorting by category or growth stage. Browsing campaigns is open to the public, more detailed information maybe only available to registered members. The investment process is only accessible to registered members.

  • Entrepreneurs

    Creating a campaign for an idea is easy and also done via the web platform. The campaign will consist of answers to questions about an idea, market, industry and team. Campaign details also include how much capital is sought, and how much equity is on offer. Investors can directly invest in all active campaigns. Creating a campaign and participating in investment is accessible to registered members only.

  • Venture Capital

    Crowd funding also known as Crowd sourcing is where small amounts of capital are collected from a large pool of individuals. These individuals are registered as 'Investors' on ABLX. The capital raised is passed on to a business owner seeking funding for expansion of an already existing operation. The recipient could also be a Venture Capitalist looking to raise seed capital for a startup.

Careful investment options

On ABLX, Entrepreneurs and their businesses are vetted prior to being listed on ABLX for compliance reasons in order to protect Investors from pretend entrepreneurs.
