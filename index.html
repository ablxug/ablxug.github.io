What is ABLX

ABLX is a private electronic platform providing registrants the facility to raise capital and or invest in start-ups and Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Africa through. We believe in using the power of the crowd and social democracy to lift Africa out of poverty, create jobs and spur development by providing capital to Entrepreneurs and SMEs. Whether you are a start-up / SME seeking capital, an Investor looking for financial returns or an agent for catalysing change and development, we provide a platform for you to satisfy your urge!

  • Deep investor network

    Crowd Funding is where capital is collected from a pool of individuals, “the Crowd”. In the case of ABLX, these individuals are registered as 'Investors'. The capital raised is passed on to an entrepreneur / business owner seeking funding to start-up, grow, expand or execute a business strategy.
    The recipients of capital are registered as 'Entrepreneurs' on ABLX and they list their entrepreneurial venture / business as a ‘Campaign’. Entrepreneurs provide equity / a share / a stake in their business in exchange for the investment received.
    For example 20% in exchange for 10,000 USD. If a pool of individuals invest 100 USD or more each, the target of 10, 000 USD could be reached and even exceeded. Each investor will receive a portion of the 20% in equity in proportion to depending on how much they have committed to the Campaign.

  • Ambitious businesses

    Using the ABLX platform for equity crowd funding provides a compliment and or supplement to debt capital. By raising capital via Crowd funding on ABLX Entrepreneurs are able to reach out not only friends, family and members of their communities but also to the wider Investor community including High Networth Individuals (HNWIs)
    , Institutional investors and other Retail Investors efficiently and at lower overall costs. In addition to raising capital, Entrepreneurs benefit from constructive criticism and feedback from Investors as part of the due diligence and review prior to capital being committed to the campaign, when a Campaign is fully funded and the capital has been raised, Investors can also contribute by opening doors for greater business sales, providing strategic advice and professional services to the business.
    For an Investor, ABLX provides an opportunity to put your financial, human and social capital towards idea, project that you believe in and gain from it at the same time. On ABLX, Entrepreneurs and their businesses are vetted prior to being listed on ABLX for fact and accuracy of all statements and assertions made in a Campaign.
